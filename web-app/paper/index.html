Description:
Deal Broker is a an application that is based on buying and selling of products for one selves or for the others. When acting as a broker, a broker/dealer executes orders .When acting as a dealer , a broker/dealer executes trades .This help people to buy/sell their products at their own cost. Deal Broker is a application which brokers between two parties. 
Party 1 - The person who wants offer something e.g. sell his car, give house for rent, Wants to go out for a date etc. here by called as Seller.
Party 2 - A person who as a need to get something e.g. buy a second hand car, get PG accommodation here by called as Buyer.
The Seller who wants to offer his services or product registers his offer through a web or mobile interface. The information gets registered and is flashed on the Deal Broker site. Similarly the Buyer who is in need, registers his need in the database.
The offers are displayed to the Requester based on his request. The offers can be visible to the Requester on the Deal Broker site as well as he can get notified through different access channels e.g. email, sms etc. The Deal Broker engine tries to marry the needs of both the parties and facilitates the deal between both of them. The main part of this project is that , it will be integrated in social networking sites like Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter etc. so that it will be very easy for the people to buy and sell their products.
Objectives:
The main objectives of this project is:
  • To use captives audience of Facebook.
  • To help people come buy and sell products at their own prices.
  • One won’t have to be in official transit, they can buy/sell offline too.
  • Earn revenue through advertisement.
  • To introduce barter system.
  • To help people in getting better deals.
